Description:

You have a huge warehouse at your disposal. there are small remarkable and at the same time unusual objects. Using them you can try to build something original and phenomenal.


KUBIKOS set is a set of low poly, cartoony, building blocks. Game contains Egypt world, Platformer world, dungeon, published to date (100+ objects) and will be added to overtime.


Features:


  • One of the best simulation games: begin constructing your dream world
  • Great visual and sound effects.
  • Add more than thousend object with out droping fps
  • Your progress never be erased
  • You can build dungeons, castle, even worlds.
  • Build with out limit!!!
